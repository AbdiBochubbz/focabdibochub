XOR Logic Gate

The operation XOR is another Boolean operation. XOR produces an output of 1 (true) when one of its inputs is 1 (true) and the other is 0 (false). For example, a statement of the form P XOR Q means “either P or Q but not both.” (In short, the XOR operation produces an output of 1 when its inputs are different.)
